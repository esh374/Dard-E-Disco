Group 1-

Dress Code

FLAME University expects students to be dressed formally, befitting the occasion, especially whenever outside the hostel rooms, and encourages them to behave responsibly.

When students are in public places in the campus the following are unacceptable:

  • Provocative or revealing clothing
  • Offensive logos/texts/symbols/images on clothes, bags, personal items or accessories
  • Clothing or accessories that may create a health or safety hazard for themselves or others

Consequences of violation of the above:

1st Instance Written warning. Students may be asked by the Faculty members to leave the class and come appropriately dressed.
2nd Instance Fine of ₹1000 + informing parents
3rd Instance Fine of ₹5000 + informing the parents + grounding
Group 2-

Public Acts of Intimacy

Obscene display of personal affection in public places is prohibited.

Consequences of violation of the above:

1st Instance Written warning.
2nd Instance Written warning + informing parents + grounding for 10 days
3rd Instance Grounding for 1 term
Group 3-

Anti Ragging Policy

Ragging is a cognizable and punishable offence. Any incident of ragging must be immediately brought to the notice of the Anti-Ragging Committee.

Any student found involved in ragging will be dealt with as per the laws of the land and Orders of the highest judicial authority of the Country, the Supreme Court of India.

It should be noted that the Maharashtra Prohibition of Ragging Act, 1999 bans all ragging activity within and outside the premises of educational institutions, student Houses and all areas frequented by students.

According to the Act cited above, “Ragging means committing an act which causes or is likely to cause insult, annoyance, fear, apprehension, threat, intimidation, outrage of modesty, injury to a student...”

Additionally, the following are also considered ragging if they:

  • Require the use of alcohol in any process or activity
  • Require nudity at any time
  • Entail vandalizing, defacing, or stealing any property, or throwing substances or objects at individuals with intent to harm them
  • Assign unreasonable chores
  • Entail verbal abuse or threats to harm
  • Force a person to act against their will.

    • Consequences of violation of the above: Expulsion from the University.

Disorderly Conduct and Harrasment

Any behaviour that disrupts the life, or violates the rights of members of the FLAME University community including any form of harassment, will be punished. Physical violence on Campus will not be tolerated.

Harassment is defined as the intimidation, invasion of privacy, or threat to the wellness of a person. This could be communicated verbally or in writing, physically or digitally through contact by telephone, computer, or a third party connection.

Consequences of violation of the above:

1st Instance Suspension for 1 term
2nd Instance Suspension for 2 terms
3rd Instance Suspension for 3 terms
